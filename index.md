# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 5-12-2025

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Information We Do Not Collect
- We do not collect names, emails, or login information.

- We do not track your activity.
- We do not use cookies or analytics tools.

## How the App Works
This app allows users to upload an Excel file containing meter coordinates.  
The app uses that file to generate a route.  
All processing happens locally on the device.  
No data is sent to any server.

## Data Sharing
We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any data.
